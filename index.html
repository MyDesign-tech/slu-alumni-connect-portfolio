üéØ Key Data Insights

Insight #1: Mentorship Seasonality

The Finding: Mentorship requests spike 35% HIGHER in Fall semester (Aug-Oct) vs. Spring. Active mentorships peak in January.

Why it matters: Students join in fall seeking career guidance before internship season. January resolutions drive sign-ups.

Action: Time mentor recruitment campaigns for August-October

Insight #2: Event RSVP Performance

The Finding: Networking mixers generate 2.3x higher RSVP rates than webinars. 70% of alumni prefer in-person vs. virtual.

Why it matters: Alumni value face-to-face networking. Traditional formats outperform virtual events at SLU.

Action: Prioritize in-person; reduce webinar frequency

Insight #3: Profile Completeness Impact

The Correlation: Alumni with >75% profile completeness show dramatically higher engagement:

  • 3.2x higher mentorship participation
  • 2.1x higher event attendance
  • 2.7x higher donation likelihood
Action: Gamify profile completion with badges
